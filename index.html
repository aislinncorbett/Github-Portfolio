Project 1

This Python project focuses on analysing supermarket sales data using libraries like NumPy, pandas, and Matplotlib. Key analytical tasks include calculating total sales over time, examining sales by product line, and evaluating customer demographics through types and gender distribution. Visualisations range from time series and bar charts to pie charts and scatter plots, providing insights into trends, customer preferences, and sales performance across different product lines and cities.
